Graphic Design

When you think about graphic design, logos, art and companies come to mind.
These are but just the beginnings of what being a graphic designer means.
As we have been told again and again by professors, any idiot can pirate photoshop
and illustrator, and call themselves a graphic designer, but that isn't
what its all about. Its not the programs you use, its not the attention to details,
its not the talent. Its all about the process, being given a project you hate with a
passion and turning it into your crowning acheivement. Its the prep time, the peer
critiques, the late nights fueled by coffee and energy drinks trying to get that last bit
done. Think on that last sentance for a minute. Doesn't that sound a little bit like
school? Thats because the graphic designers job isnt just to create something
someone else can be proud of, its about learning new things to make people happy, to
give them a glow that only that perfect logo or design, or drawing can.
Its all about learning, even our professors have admitted to learning meriads or new
and unique things from students. Things that they will use to create other things
things that make them happy. Graphic design isnt just a program you use, or a career
path, It's a unique perspective on life, something that makes you notice everything
and learn anything from anyone. its... Fun.
